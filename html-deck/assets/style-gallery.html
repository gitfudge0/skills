Marginalia

human · annotated minimal

Ink on warm paper, one oversized grotesk, and a single marker color that only ever appears as a hand-drawn mark — a circled word, an underline, an arrow. Everything else stays quiet. The annotation layer is the personality; the page reads like a thoughtful person marked up a clean document.

Verdant

friendly product deck

A fresh, optimistic product look: soft rounded cards on a tinted ground, a confident green that signals "go/healthy", tabular metric numerals, and a dotted-tile motif. Structure is shown with pills and circular marks. Reads modern and approachable without losing rigor.

03

Blueprint

technical schematic

A draughtsman's drawing: faint graph-paper grid, a cool engineering blue, monospaced annotations, and dimension lines that "measure" the headline. The structure literally looks like a spec sheet — apt for a system that is itself about tracing and measurement. Dark mode is a true blueprint (light ink on deep blue).

Editorial

magazine

A magazine feature, not a memo: a large italic display serif, a single hot accent, a drop cap, and hairline column rules. The voice is confident and considered. Distinct from the others — expressive italic serif with a vivid magenta.

Terminal

console

The report dressed as the thing it documents: a monospaced console with window chrome, a command prompt, a blinking caret, and bracketed [status] tags. Phosphor-green on black in dark mode, an ink-on-paper terminal in light. On-theme for a backend audit log; respects reduced-motion.

Brutalist

hard offset shadows

Loud and structural: thick black borders, hard offset shadows with zero blur, clashing primaries (electric blue + highlighter yellow), and sticker-style badges. Every block feels physical and stacked. Best when the report should feel energetic and unmistakably not a default template.

Aurora Glass

frosted glass · gradient mesh

Atmospheric and modern: frosted translucent cards float over a soft aurora gradient mesh, with a violet-to-cyan accent that also tints the headline. Light mode is bright and airy; dark mode glows. The surfaces are see-through and the background is alive with color.
